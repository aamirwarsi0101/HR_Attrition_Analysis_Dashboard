# HR Analytics Dashboard – Power BI

## Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze workforce trends, employee attrition, job satisfaction, demographic distribution, and departmental performance. The dashboard enables HR teams and business stakeholders to identify key attrition drivers and make data-driven workforce decisions.

---

## Objectives

* Monitor employee attrition and retention trends.
* Analyze workforce demographics and distribution.
* Identify factors influencing employee turnover.
* Evaluate job satisfaction across the organization.
* Support strategic HR decision-making through actionable insights.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Employees: 10,000
* Attrition Count: 2,000
* Attrition Rate: 22%
* Average Employee Age: 40 Years

* Gender Distribution

* Age Group Analysis

### Employee Satisfaction Analysis

## Key Insights

* Employees with lower job satisfaction show higher attrition rates.
* Certain departments contribute significantly to overall attrition.
* Employee age groups and commuting distance influence retention patterns.
* Workforce demographics provide valuable insights into organizational structure.
